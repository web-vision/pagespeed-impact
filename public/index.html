Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify the pagespeed that google measures for my website?

Head over to https://pagespeed.web.dev/ and enter the domain or URL of your website or online-store. Wait a couple of seconds to let google measure your website. Once google is done, find the Speed Index for your site in seconds. Also, make sure to measure your site for mobile.

How can I see over a period of time, how google is measuring the performance of my site?

Google measures the performance of your website over time, based on real-world Chrome users experience. You can find these data on the Website https://cruxvis.withgoogle.com/. Also, this page gives you a tendency how your site is evolving over time.
